    • Santosh Achary Vishwakarma Satya kaha Acharya ji.. iss kalyug me Satya Sanatan Dharm ki gehrayi ko samajhane ke liye bahut se Yogi vifal ho gaye hai aur wo Sanatan Dharm ko har yug me naye naye dhang se apani suvidha ke anusar paribhashit karte hai.. Acharya ji, Swamy Ramdev ke product mai bhi prayog karta hu, lekin unaki har bato se mai sehmat nahi rehata hu. Aapane swamy Ramdev ko shastro aur itihas ka agyani bataya wo bhi kuch had tak thik hai kyunki Sanatan Dharm ek mahasagar hai isake gyan ko purnataya koi prapt nahi kar sakata aur itihas bhi kisi mahasagar se kam nahi hai. Lekin aapane swamy Ramdev ko jatiya vaimasya se pidit bataya. Swamy Ramdev to janm se Yaduvanshi the agar mai sahi hu to wo dwij hone ke bad se Brahman huye Aur Jagatguru Adi Shankaracharya bhi kul se aur karm se brahman hi the.. unaki biography jinhone likhi unake anusar shankaracharya jab Digvijay hokar aur Jagatguru ki upadhi pakar dakshin bharat pahiche tab unako kisi ne pucha aapaki kya pehchan hai to unhone gayan ke madhyam se bataya ki " Acharya namo shankara, twastha putro na sanshay, viprakule gururdiksha Vishwakarmantu brahmana" ( book- shankara vijaya) . kya ye book ka quot satya hai shankaracharya ki jo jati batayi gayi hai aur jo kul bataya gaya hai wo sab satya hai. Kripaya Acharya ji iss sanskrit ke kathan ko hindi me samjhane ki kripa karenge.. ? agar shankaracharya ji aur Swamy Ramdev ji jo bad me karm se brahman bane wo jatiya vaimasyata ke karan adi shankara ko kuch jagah galat siddh karte hai to wo sarasar galat hai aur anuchit hai. Mujhe to pata tha ki Aarya samaji vaidik vidwan jaise shankaracharya ko samman ki dristhi se dekhate hai lekin mujhe aaj pata chala ki Ramdev ji jaise log unpar aarop lagate hai. Ye anuchit hai. Jisako Sanatan Dharm ka Surya kaha jata ho ( Adi Shankaracharya) unake bare me koi bhi bat sunana aur bardast karna sarasar galat hi hoga.. unaki bate jaise " Brahm Satyam Jagat mithya" ko samjhana kuch hi kalyugi bahmano aur anya logo ke liye sambhav hai.. Acharya ji jaisa ki aap kehate hai ki brahman jo karmkandi hai wo Sirf bhikshatan se hi apani jivika chalane ka uttaradhikari hota hai agar wo aur kisi tarike se dhanoparjan karta hai to wo chandal shreni ka adhikari hota hai.. isase mai shat pratishat sehmat hu.. Acharya ji , meri aapase prarthana hai ki Jagatguru Adi Shankarcharya ke jivini aur unake pramukh karmo ke bare me kuch sankshipt lekh likhe to aapaka rini rahunga.. Pranam...
