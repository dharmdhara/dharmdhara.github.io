  • Narasimha Rao There was a conversation between Rajarshi Janaka and Brahmarshi Rishyasringa. The latter told the former that cow ghee is ideal for yajna. IF it is unavailable, he said buffalo ghee can be used. IF it is also unavailable, he said goat ghee can be used. IF it is also unavailable, he said sesame oil can be used. IF it is also unavailable, he said mustard oil can be used. IF it is also unavailable, he said ANY oil can be used.

    Bottomline: Rishis are compassionate. They always gave plenty of options. Though there are preferred materials, they never said "must".
